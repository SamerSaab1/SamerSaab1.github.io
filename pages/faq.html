Frequently Asked Questions

Q1. Why isn’t this list integrated into ORBIT conference grades?

Because ORBIT conference grades are designed for proceedings-centric venues. Applying the same grading logic to non-proceedings meetings would introduce systematic bias.

Q2. Are these meetings “better” than proceedings conferences?

No. They represent different scholarly ecosystems. Many disciplines communicate impact through meetings without proceedings.

Q3. Why are some famous meetings missing?

Inclusion requires stable, verifiable public evidence and review. Some meetings have not yet been reviewed or lack stable documentation.

Q4. Can a meeting appear in both ORBIT and this list?

Yes in principle, but overlap is rare because the publication ecosystems differ.

Q5. Will this list be updated?

Yes. The list is versioned and may expand as evidence-backed suggestions are added.

Q6. What if a prestigious meeting I recognize is not included here?

This list is not exhaustive. Absence does not imply lower standing; it may simply mean the meeting has not yet been reviewed or lacks stable public evidence. Evidence-backed suggestions are welcome.

Micro-disclaimer: This list is a contextual reference, not a ranking—no scores, tiers, or implied ordering.
