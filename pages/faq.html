Q1. Why isn’t this list integrated into ORBIT conference grades?

Because ORBIT grades are designed for proceedings-centric venues. Applying the same logic here would introduce bias.

Q2. Are these meetings “better” than proceedings conferences?

No. They represent different scholarly ecosystems.

Q3. Why are some famous meetings missing?

Inclusion requires stable, verifiable evidence and review; some meetings have not yet been reviewed.

Q4. Can a meeting appear in both ORBIT and this list?

Yes in principle, but overlap is rare.

Q5. Will this list be updated?

Yes. The list is versioned and may expand as evidence-backed suggestions are added.

Q6. What if a prestigious meeting I recognize is not included here?

This list is not exhaustive. Absence does not imply lower standing; it may simply mean the meeting has not yet been reviewed or lacks stable public evidence. Evidence-backed suggestions are welcome.
