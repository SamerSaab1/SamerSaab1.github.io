About the ORBIT Non-Proceedings Meetings List

The ORBIT Non-Proceedings Meetings List is a curated, discipline-balanced catalog of prestigious scholarly meetings whose primary scholarly record is not an archival full-paper proceedings volume.

Many leading meetings in medicine and health, biology and life sciences, physical and mathematical sciences, business and economics, social sciences, and the humanities disseminate research through peer-reviewed abstracts, journal abstract supplements, paper repositories / working paper series, or program and panel archives.
