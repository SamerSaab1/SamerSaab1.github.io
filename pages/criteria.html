Selection Criteria (Strict)

1) Non-Proceedings Criterion (Required)

The meeting does not publish peer-reviewed full papers in a dedicated proceedings volume or series as its primary scholarly output.

  • Journal abstract supplements
  • Abstract books / abstract archives
  • Paper repositories / working paper series
  • Program or panel archives

2) Prestige / Standing Criterion (Required)

At least one of the following applies:

  • Flagship annual or biennial meeting of a major international scholarly society
  • Widely recognized venue for first disclosure of major results
  • Competitive peer selection of abstracts or papers
  • Strong international or top-regional scope (Europe, Asia-Pacific, global)

3) Evidence Requirement (Required)

Every entry includes an Evidence URL pointing to at least one of:

  • Official meeting page
  • Abstract submission/archive page
  • Journal supplement page
  • Society policy page confirming non-proceedings record

4) Confidence Classification

  • High: clear, well-documented non-proceedings record
  • Medium: strong venue, but documentation is less explicit
