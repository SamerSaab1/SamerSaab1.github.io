Selection Criteria (Strict)

1) Non-Proceedings Criterion (Required)

The meeting does not publish peer-reviewed full papers in a dedicated proceedings volume or series as its primary scholarly output.

  • Journal abstract supplements
  • Abstract books / abstract archives
  • Paper repositories / working paper series
  • Program or panel archives

2) Prestige / Standing Criterion (Required)

  • Flagship meeting of a major society, and/or
  • Venue for first disclosure of major results, and/or
  • Competitive peer selection, and/or
  • Strong international or top-regional scope

3) Evidence Requirement (Required)

Each entry includes an Evidence URL pointing to an official meeting page, abstract archive/submission page, journal supplement page, or society policy page.

4) Confidence Classification

  • High: clear and well-documented non-proceedings record.
  • Medium: strong meeting, but documentation is less explicit.
